Microwave Caramel Popcorn (vegan)

 

Combine:

 

1 stick margarine

½ C brown sugar

¼ C light karo syrup

 

Cook in microwave 2 ½ minutes on High.  Remove and stir.  Cook 2 minutes on high and stir.  Add ½ t. baking soda and stir.  Spray large brown paper bag.  Add 12 C popped popcorn (already made popcorn either from scratch or plain microwave popcorn bag).  Add peanuts if desired. Pour mixture over popcorn.  Shake bag.  Place in microwave.  Cook on high 1 ½ minutes.  Shake.  Cook on high 1 ½ minutes.  Shake. Cook on high 30 sec.  Shake.  Spread on greased pan or wax paper.  Let cool, then eat!
