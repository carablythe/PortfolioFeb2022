Cheesecake   (vegetarian version, but still working on a vegan version, sorry :(  )

Ingredients:

Cream cheese layer:

3x  200g (8 oz.)  packets of cream cheese (softened)

2 large eggs

2 t vanilla extract

1C sugar 

Crust:

16 cookies/10 graham crackers –make sure they’re finely ground

8T melted butter or margarine 

Sour cream layer:

1/2C sugar

2t vanilla extract

2x  200ml containers of crème fraiche OR  1x  16 oz. sour cream   (Make sure the sour cream doesn’t contain rennet for the vegetarian version)

 

Directions:

Mix cream cheese layer ingredients in mixing bowl until smooth and set aside.

Purée graham crackers or cookies. Then work butter into cookie crumbs with a spoon or your fingers. Carefully press crust mixture into cheesecake pan, raising the crust to the sides of the pan as well.

Pour in the cream cheese mixture, and smooth out the top with a spatula. Bake at 375°F/190°C for about 15-20 minutes or until the top of the cream cheese layer becomes light brown.

In the meantime, prepare the top sour cream/crème fraiche layer in another bowl and set aside.

Once the bottom layer has finished cooking, check it by doing a touch test to see if it’s pouncy. Let cool for ten minutes.

Pour top layer over cooked layer and cook an additional 10-15 minutes or so on 425°F/220°C. It’s okay if it is still kind of liquidy when you take it out of the oven (because it will congeal in the refrigerator). Let cool and refrigerate several hours until cheesecake is firm.  Serve topped with fresh berries or a berry glaze (use the berry pie filling recipe with your berry of choice), if you so desire.
