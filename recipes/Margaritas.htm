Traditional Frozen Margaritas


Ingredients:

 

Directions:

·         1/3 cup tequila

·         1/3 cup lime juice

·         1/4 cup sugar

·         2 ½ cups crushed ice

·         salt, to coat the rim of the glasses

·         1 lime, cut in slices  

 

1. Dip the rims wet champagne glasses in the salt. Apply evenly.

2.Add tequila, lime juice, sugar and crushed iced to a blender or food processor. Blend for about 15 seconds.

3. Pour the mixture into the glass, and garnish with a lime slice.

 

Frozen Margaritas (Fruit of Choice)


Ingredients:

 

Directions:

 

·          1/3 cup tequila

·          2 cups frozen fruit

·          1 cup crushed ice

·          1 tablespoon lemon juice

·          sugar, to taste

·          thinly sliced fruit or mint leaves, as desired for garnish

 

 


1. Add the tequila, frozen fruit, crushed ice, lemon juice and sugar (if needed) to a blender or food processor.  Blend for about 15 seconds.
2. Pour the mixture into the glass, and garnish with a slice of fruit.
