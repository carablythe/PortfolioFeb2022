Teriyaki (with vegetarian and vegan versions)

 

Chicken, salmon, mushrooms and eggplant are excellent teriyaki style, but use whatever meat or vegetable you think would be good.

 

Mix together:

 

1/3 C soy sauce

1/3 C sugar

1/3 C mirin (cooking rice wine)…sake can be used instead, if you can’t find mirin

 

Pour a little sauce in the frying pan, turn on medium high and add the meat or veggie.  Cook briefly and then add the rest of the sauce.  Cook until meat or vegetable is cooked through and sauce starts to form a glaze (turn on high for awhile if sauce does not start glazing).
