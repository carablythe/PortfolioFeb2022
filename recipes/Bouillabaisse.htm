BOUILLABAISSE

Ingredients:

 

500 grams shrimp

500 grams white fish (any)

500 grams mussels or clams

1 can diced tomatoes

1 pinch of saffron

6 T olive oil

1 onion, sliced

1 leek, diced

1 stalk celery, diced

2 cloves garlic, diced

1 pack bouquet garni

1 teaspoon orange zest

½ teaspoon fennel seeds

¼ cup white wine

salt and pepper, to taste

3 T parsley

1 teaspoon fish stock (if the soup is not strong enough)

5 cups water

1 loaf French bread

 

Directions:

 

1.     Cut the fish into bite-sized cubes

2.     Put the saffron in two tablespoons of hot water and steep.

3.     Heat the oil on medium heat in a large pot. Add the onions, leek, celery and cook until soft and translucent.

4.     Add the garlic, bouquet garni, orange zest, fennel, tomato, saffron and hot water, and 5 cups of water to the pot. Season with salt and pepper.

5.     Bring to a boil and then simmer on low hear for 30-40 minutes.

6.     Add the shellfish to the pot and boil on high heat for 6 minutes.

7.     Put in the fish and cook another 6 minutes.

8.     Add the white wine (and dashi) and simmer until ready to eat.

9.     Serve in soup bowls with parsley sprinkled on top and with some French bread.
