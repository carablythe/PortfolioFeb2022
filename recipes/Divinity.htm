Grandma’s Divinity Candy (vegetarian)

 

2 ¾ C sugar

¾ C white karo

¾ C water

2 egg whites

1 C chopped nuts

1 t vanilla

 

Cook sugar, karo, and water until it cracks in cold water.  Pour ¾ of the mixture into beaten egg whites, heating all the time.  Set the remaining ¼ of the mixture back on the fire and cook another minute.  Then pour that into the egg white mixture.  Beat until you can longer use the electric mixture.  Then add vanilla and pecans.  Use a spoon to beat until it is ready to pour into a large greased platter (1/2” –1” thickness).  Cut the candy into 1”x1” squares before it gets too hard, but be sure it is firm.
