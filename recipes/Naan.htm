Naan  (vegetarian with vegan version)

 

 

Mix:

2 C flour

½ t salt

1 ¼ t active dry yeast

 

Then add:

2 T melted butter or vegetable/olive oil for vegan version

¾ C yogurt or buttermilk, room temperature  (1/2 C tofu for vegan version)

1 t - 1 T water, as needed

 

Knead mixture thoroughly.  Make the dough smooth and keep aside for several hours in a warm place.  Divide dough into equal parts and shape into tennis-like balls.  Flatten like pancakes.  Brush a little olive oil on top.  Sprinkle salt.  Put on the grill and oil the other side.  The more oil, the better.
