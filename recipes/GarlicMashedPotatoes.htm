                       Garlic Mashed Potatoes 

 

Ingredients:

　

á     12 white potatoes

á     1/2 C butter

á     1/4 C milk

á     1 T garlic powder/minced garlic

á     Salt and pepper, to taste

 

Directions:

 

1.    Clean, peel and boil 12 potatoes for 5 minutes in a large pot.

2.    Change to medium heat for 20 minutes.

3.    Drain the water and add butter, milk, and garlic powder/minced garlic to the pot.

4.    Blend with a mixer until smooth.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
