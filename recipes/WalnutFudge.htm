Chocolate Walnut Fudge

Ingredients:

 

1 lb. (16-oz/2 cups/450g) dark chocolate chips or chocolate bars

1 ½ cups (12-fl.oz./350ml) heavy cream

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup chopped walnuts

1 T vegetable oil

8-inch (20cm) square glass cake pan/ Corningware or equivalent size rectangular glass pan, lightly greased with softened butter

 

Directions:

1.    Grease the inside of a heavy pot with vegetable oil.

2.    Add chocolate and cream and cook over medium heat.

3.    Stir until chocolate is melted, cream combined, and starts to get very thick.

4.    Remove fudge from heat and stir in vanilla and walnuts.

5.    Spoon fudge into greased glass pan.

6.    The fudge will take at least one hour to set.  Place in a cool place to speed up the process.

7.    Once hardened, slice the fudge into small cubes. (Makes about 48 pieces.)
