Pound Cake 

(with Berry Sauce and Whipped Cream)

Cake ingredients:

 

1 ½ cups sugar

2 cups flour

1 cup margarine or butter

4 eggs

1 tablespoon vanilla extract

1 small pack silken tofu

Paper/foil cupcake holders or other cake mold of your choice  

 

Berry sauce ingredients:

　

1.5-2 cups frozen berries

1/2 C sugar

1/2 C water　

Cake directions:

 

1.    Preheat oven to 180°C.

2.    Add all of the cake ingredients to a large mixing bowl and blend for 10 minutes with an electric mixer on medium speed.

3.    Carefully spoon mixture into greased cake holders. 　　　　　　　

4.    Bake for 20 minutes before checking for doneness. 　　　　　　　

 

Berry sauce directions:

 

1.    In a small pot, boil the water, sugar and berries.

2.    Let the mixture cook down.

3.    Once the cake cools, top with berry sauce and whipped cream.
