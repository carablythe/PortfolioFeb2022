Vegan Banana Bread

 

1 C mashed ripe bananas (about 2 or 3)

¼ C soymilk or water

1/3 C vegetable oil

2 C of all-purpose flour

1 C sugar

2 t baking powder

¼ t salt

½ C chopped nuts

 

Mix all of the above ingredients together.

 

Recommended pan: 9”x5” loaf pan

Temperature: 350°

Time Cook:  20-25 minutes

 

Source:  Donna Phillips
