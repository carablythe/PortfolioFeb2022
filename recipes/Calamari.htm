Fried Calamari

　

Ingredients:

 

á       vegetable oil, for deep-frying

á       1 pound (450g) squid with tentacles, cut into 1-2cm -thick rings

á       2 cups all-purpose flour 

á       2 tablespoons parsley (fresh or dried)  

á       salt and pepper, to taste   

 

Directions:

 

1.Pour enough oil into a heavy large pot to reach the depth of 7 cm. Heat over medium heat for a few minutes.

 

2. Mix the flour, parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Working in small batches, toss the squid into the flour mixture to coat. 　

 

3. Carefully add the squid to the oil and fry until crisp and pale golden, about 1 minute per batch.

 

4.Using tongs, transfer the fried calamari to a paper-towel lined plate to drain.

 

5. Place the fried calamari and lemon wedges on a plate. Sprinkle with salt. Serve with marinara sauce.
