Empanadas

Filling Ingredients:

 

1 can refried beans or black beans OR 300-400 g ground beef

1 medium onion, diced

1 T vegetable oil

1 tsp. cumin

1/8 tsp. ground cayenne pepper

salt, to taste

 

Pie Crust Ingredients:

 

2 cups flour + about ½ cup flour for rolling the dough

1 tsp. salt   

1/3 cup cold water 

2/3 cup butter or margarine

Filling Directions:　

 

1.  Sauté the diced onions (and the beef if you use it) in the vegetable oil in a large pot.　　　

2.  Once the onions are translucent (and beef is thoroughly cooked), set aside.　

3.  If you use beans, drain any liquid from the can and add it to the pot with the onions. 　

4.  Season the mixture with the cumin, cayenne and salt and mix thoroughly with a spoon. Set aside.

 

Pie Crust Directions:　

 

1.  Mix together 2 cups flour and 1 tsp salt.   

2.  Mix that with 1/3 cup cold water to make a paste. 

3.  Then cut in 2/3 cup butter/margarine until pea-sized balls form. 

4.  Cover both hands in flour.

5.  With your hands, form the dough into ping-pong sized balls.

6.  Roll the dough out on a floured surface with a rolling pin to make a 5~8 cm circle.

7.  Add the filling to the crusts and fold to make a half-moon shape.

8.  Pinch the edges of the piecrust together to close tightly.

9.  Bake at 200° for 15 minutes or until golden brown.

10. Remove from the oven to let cool. 

 11. Serve alone or with salsa, sour cream, and/or guacamole on top.
