Spiced Tea  (vegan)

 

2 1/3 C sugar

2 C Tang

1 t cinnamon

1 C instant tea

2 pkgs Kool-Aid Lemonade Mix (unsweetened)

¼ t ground cloves

 

Combine all ingredients.  Best if put in blender (half of recipe at a time).  Use 2-3 T of this mixture per cup of hot water.

 

 

Put this mixture in a tin or jar, wrap a ribbon around it, and you’ve got a lovely Christmas gift!

 

 

Adapted from Source:  LaBeth Pondish, LaPort Independent School District Cookbook
