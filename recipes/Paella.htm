Spanish Seafood Paella

 

Ingredients:

 

1/4 cup olive oil   

2 garlic cloves, crushed　　

1/2 onion, diced    

2 tomatoes, finely chopped 

Salt and black pepper, to taste   　

1 teaspoon dried oregano   　

½ tsp. paprika powder

2 cups rice  

a pinch of saffron threads 

4 cups warm chicken stock  

250g squid, cut in rings   

250g jumbo shrimp

8 mussels  or 12 clams  

1/2 cup sweet peas       

1 lemon, cut in wedges    

½ red and ½ yellow pepper, cut in strips   

 

Directions:

 

1. Heat the oil in a large pot. Sauté the garlic, onion, and tomatoes, cook until the mixture browns. Season with salt, black pepper, oregano and paprika. 

2. Add the rice and stir. Steep the saffron in 1/4-cup hot water for 5 minutes then stir it into the rice.　　

3. Add the stock and simmer for 10 minutes。Do not cover or constantly stir.

4. Add the squid, shrimp and clams/mussels. Simmer, without stirring, until the rice is al dente and the mussels have opened. (Do not eat any mussels that do not.)

5.Scatter the peas and bell peppers on top and continue to cook until the liquid is absorbed.

6. Set aside, off the heat, for 5 minutes before serving. Garnish with lemon wedges.
