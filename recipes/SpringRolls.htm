Fresh Thai Spring Rolls (vegan)

 

Ingredients:

 

1 package of Rice paper sheets (large, preferably)

1 small package of vermicelli (clear rice noodles)

1 small bunch of bean sprouts

1 small package/bunch of enoki or other small mushrooms

Half of a carrot, diced matchstick-style

Half of a cucumber, diced matchstick-style

Leaf lettuce (tear 1/4 of a leaf per spring roll)

A small bunch of fresh cilantro

Boiled shrimp (1/2 pound or so…omit for vegan version)

Thai Sweet Chili Sauce

 

Directions:< 30 mins AppetizersThai Appetizers

Chicken Breasts Appetizers

1.      Boil vermicelli several minutes, until soft. Remove from heat and place in bowl.

2.    Assemble other ingredients on counter or table in bowls.

3.    Soak 1 rice paper in luke warm water until soft, no longer than 15 seconds (or they will get too soggy) and lay on a plate.

4.    Layer noodle mix, veggies and shrimp on top of rice paper (as desired, but remember that you have to roll this all up inside).

5.    Carefully start to roll, tucking in the sides, then continue to roll up. If not sturdy enough, wrap another soft rice paper sheet around it.

6.    Serve immediately. (It’s also fun to just have all the ingredients laid out and to roll with friends right before eating).  Dip in Chili Sauce.
