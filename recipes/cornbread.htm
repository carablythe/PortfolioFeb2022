                                   Cornbread

 

Ingredients:

　

á    2 C corn meal/ corn flour 

á    2 C flour

á    1/2 C sugar

á    2 T baking powder 

á    2 tsp salt

á    2 C  butter/margarine (softened)

á    1 C sour milk (add ½ tsp. vinegar) 　

á    1 package silken tofu  (about 150g)

á    2 eggs

 

 

Directions:

 

1.Blend egg, tofu and milk together.

 

2. Blend in cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, margarine and salt.

 

3. Pour into a large greased cake pan and bake for 20-25 minutes @190 degrees.
