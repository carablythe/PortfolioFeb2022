Peppermint Bark

  

Ingredients:

 

12 oz. (340g) Dark chocolate chips or bars

 

12 oz. (340g) White chocolate chips or bars

 

6 (84 g) candy canes or equivalent amount of peppermint candy

 

Directions:

 

1.    Put the candy canes/peppermint candy (without plastic wrap) in a plastic or Ziploc bag and close tightly. Bang with a mallet, hammer, rolling pin or the like until small (½ cm) pieces.

 

2.    Put the dark chocolate in a bowl and microwave for 45 seconds; then stir thoroughly. Repeat heating and stirring 2~3 times until completely melted.

 

3.    Pour out onto a greased large sheet of wax or parchment paper and spread evenly with a spatula into a large, thin (0.5cm~1cm high) rectangle.

 

4.    Let cool completely (about 1 hour).

 

5.    Now, put the white chocolate in a bowl and microwave over high heat for 45 seconds; then stir thoroughly. Repeat this step 2~3 times until completely melted.

 

6.    Pour the white chocolate on top of the dark chocolate layer to make another 0.5-1cm thin layer of white chocolate, and spread with a spatula gently and evenly. (It is okay if some of the dark chocolate mixes, and the white chocolate layer marbleizes.)

 

7.    Without waiting, sprinkle the peppermint pieces (and any leftover candy powder), covering the white chocolate layer. Push down on the peppermint gently so that they are somewhat pushed into the white chocolate.

 

8.    Let cool completely (about 1 hour) and break into pieces (3~6cm squares).
