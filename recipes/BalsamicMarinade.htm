Balsamic Vinegar Marinade (vegetarian/vegan)

 

This is great with salmon or tuna steaks (and other seafood, I imagine), chicken breasts, mushrooms, eggplant, what have you.

 

 

2 t soy sauce

 

1 ½ - 2 T balsamic vinegar

 

1 ½ T honey (or 2 t sugar for the vegan version)

 

2-3 cloves minced garlic

 

½ - 1 t grated fresh ginger or dried ginger

 

1 T white cooking wine

 

2 t vegetable oil (I use olive)

 

2 t  lemon or lime juice

 

 

Mix the above ingredients and let the meat and/or veggies sit in the marinade for at least 30 minutes.  Best if cooked in the marinade on aluminum foil on the grill.
