¡Guacamole!

Ingredients:

 

·      3 avocados  

·      1 T lemon juice

·      1 T of ground onion or onion powder 　

·      salt, to taste

·      1 clove of garlic, minced or 1 t garlic powder (optional)　

·      1 large tomato, diced (optional)

 

Directions:

 

1.Cut the avocados, put them in a bowl, mash them with a fork, then put in the seeds.

 

2. Add the other ingredients and mix gently with a fork.

 

3. Use as a dip with tortilla chips or topping for other Mexican dishes.
