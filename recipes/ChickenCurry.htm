Japanese Hot Chicken Curry over Rice

 

Recipe Ingredients:

 

2-3 C cooked rice

1 package curry sauce mix (S&B Golden Curry or other brand)

2-3 lbs. chicken

6-7 cups of water

5 cubed potatoes

1 diced onion

1 cup of frozen sweet peas

 

In a large stew pot, add the pork or chicken and cover with water (about 6 cups). Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and cook until tender.  Remove meat from broth and cut into cubes.  Separate oil from broth, if desired. Add the curry mix to the broth (adding additional water, if necessary).  Stir until curry cubes are completely dissolved.  Add meat, potatoes, and onions.  Simmer until potatoes are cooked (20 minutes or so).  Add frozen peas.  Stir and cook 5 more minutes.  Serve on a bed of rice.
