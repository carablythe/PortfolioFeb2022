Apple Pie       (vegetarian with vegan version)

 

Crust:

Mix together 2 cups flour and 1 tsp salt.  Take out 1/3 cup of this mixture and mix that with 1/3 cup cold water to make a paste.  To the remaining mixture (w/o the water) cut in 2/3 cup butter (or margarine for vegan version) until pea-sized balls form.  Finally, mix in the flour paste.  Form into a ball, cover in wax paper, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

 

Filling:

1 tsp cinnamon

¼ tsp nutmeg

pinch of salt

1 ½ T corn starch

2/3 cup sugar

6-8 apples

 

[Put cereal in the bottom of the crust before adding apple mixture.  Before adding the top crust, dot the apples with butter (or margarine for vegan version).]

 

Bake at 475º for 15 minutes, then at 350º for 45-60 minutes.
