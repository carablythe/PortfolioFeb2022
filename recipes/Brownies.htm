BROWNIES

 

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pack tofu (optional – for cakey, not chewy brownies)
  • 1 ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts and/or or 1 pack melted cream cheese (optional)

 

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees. Grease a baking pan.
  2. Mix all ingredients except last in the large mixing bowl.  Then, add the walnuts and/or cream cheese and swirl in.
  3. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the brownie begins to pull away from edges of pan. Let cool on a wire rack before cutting into squares.
