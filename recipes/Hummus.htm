Hummus (vegan)

 

1 can of already cooked garbanzo beans/ chick peas, mostly drained but with a little liquid left

 

1 – 1 ½ T tahini paste  (try looking near the peanut butter or in international foods section)

 

1 T lemon juice (or more, if you wish)

 

1 glove of garlic

 

salt, to taste

 

Blend all of the above ingredients together in a food processor or blender until smooth and even “fluffy” or “airy”. 

 

For kicks, you might also consider adding a little roasted red pepper or fresh basil.

 

 

Serve with pita bread or use as a raw vegetable or chip dip.  Also great with falafel!
