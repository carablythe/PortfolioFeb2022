Strawberry Pie

 

 

Pie filling ingredients:

2 packs of strawberries, sliced

2/3 cup sugar

1/2 cup water

1 ½ Tbsp. cornstarch

A few drops red food coloring

1~2 tsp. lemon juice (if you like it tart)

 

Pie crust ingredients:

2 cups flour + about ½ cup flour for rolling the dough

1 tsp. salt   

1/3 cup cold water  

2/3 cup butter or margarine   　

 

Pie filling directions:

1.  Heat the water in a medium pot.

2.  Add the cornstarch. Stir well so it does not harden.

3.  Add the sugar and half of the strawberries (about 1 pack).

4.  Add the lemon juice (if you like).

5.  Let the mixture cook down.

6.  Next, add the food coloring and stir well.

7.  Then add the rest of the strawberries and stir gently.

 

Pie crust directions:

1.  Mix together 2 cups flour and 1 tsp salt.    

2.  Mix that with 1/3 cup cold water to make a paste. 

3.  Then cut in 2/3 cup butter/margarine until pea-sized balls form. 

4.  Cover both hands in flour.

5.  With your hands, form the dough into ping-pong sized balls.

6.  Roll the dough out on a floured surface with a rolling pin to make a 5~8 cm circle.

7.  Once the filling is cool, add to the crusts and fold to make a half-moon shape.

8.  Pinch the edges of the piecrust together to close tightly.

9.  Bake at 200¡ for 15 minutes or until golden brown.

10.        Remove from the oven to let cool. 

11.        Serve alone or with vanilla ice cream.
