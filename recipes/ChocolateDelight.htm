Chocolate Delight (sorry, no vegetarian/vegan versions at this time)

 

Ingredients:

 

1 C flour

1 stick margarine, melted

1 C chopped pecans

1 C powdered sugar

1 large Cool Whip (or 2 C of whipped cream, if you so dare)

1  8-oz. cream cheese

2  3-oz. instant chocolate pudding

3 C milk

½ C mini chocolate chips or chocolate shavings

 

Directions:

 

Mix first 3 ingredients and press out into 9x13 baking dish.  Bake 15 minutes at 350 degrees. While this is cooling, beat until smooth: powdered sugar, ½ Cool Whip and cream cheese.  Spread this over cooled crust.  Beat pudding and milk until thick.  Then spread over cream cheese mixture.  Top with remaining Cool Whip and garnish with chocolate chips/chocolate shavings.

 

 

 

Source: Debbie Blackburn, Colony Ben PTO Cookbook
