Tiramisu   (vegetarian)

 

Mix together the Filling ingredients:

 

1 ½ C powder sugar

¼ C Kahlua

8 oz. Mascarpone cheese (or cream cheese, if you can’t find Mascarpone)

 

After mixing the above ingredients, slowly add 1 ½ C heavy whipping cream to the mixture.

 

Divide a pack of about 30 ladyfingers in half (or divide the Easy Pound Cake into 30 pieces)

 

Mix together:

1 c boiling water

½ C espresso or really strong coffee

¼ C Kahlua

½ C sugar

 

Pour about 1 T of this mixture in a 9x9 glass pan (or similar-sized baking container).  Dip half of the ladyfingers quickly in this mixture, and lay them side-by-side on the bottom of the pan in an orderly fashion, leaving no empty space.

 

Scoop half of the Filling out and spread carefully over the layer of ladyfingers. 

 

Sprinkle a little of the coffee mixture over the Filling layer, and repeat with another layer of the remaining ladyfingers, dipping quickly, then laying side-by-side in an orderly fashion, and topping with the rest of the Filling.

 

If you have some remaining coffee mixture, sprinkle a little more on the top of the second Filling layer. 

 

A final dusting of a little cocoa and/or chocolate bar shavings (milk, dark, white) on top is ideal.

 

Refrigerate at least one hour before serving.

 

 

Source:  Beth Etter

 

Tiramisu – Olive Garden Recipe   (vegetarian)

 

Ingredients

·         SPONGE CAKE, 10-12", 3" tall

·         STRONG BLACK COFFEE OR PREPARED INSTANT ESPRESSO, 3 oz.

·         BRANDY OR RUM, 3 ounces

·         MASCARPONE OR CREAM CHEESE, 1-1/2 pounds at room temperature

·         SUPERFINE OR POWDERED SUGAR, 1-1/2 cups

·         UNSWEETENED COCOA POWDER, enough to dust

Directions

1.     Cut across the middle of the sponge cake to form two disks, about 1" to 1-1/2" thick.

2.    Blend the coffee or espresso and liqueur together.

3.    Sprinkle the bottom half of the cake with the coffee liqueur blend, enough to flavor it strongly, but don't saturate the cake so much it will collapse.

4.    Mix the cream cheese or mascarpone with the sugar, and beat until the sugar is completely dissolved and the cheese is light and spreadable.

5.    Spread the bottom half of the cake with half of the whipped cheese, in a fairly thick layer.

6.    Set the second half of the cake on the bottom half, and repeat the process.

7.     Sprinkle the coffee/liqueur blend and spread with the remaining whipped cheese mixture.

8.    Put the cocoa powder into a wire strainer and coat the top layer of the cheese completely with cocoa.

9.    Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before cutting and serving

Emeril Lagasse’s Sponge Cake Recipe

 

Ingredients: Hot Milk Sponge

  • MILK, 1/4 cup
  • BUTTER, 2 teaspoons butter
  • FLOUR, 1-1/4 cups
  • BAKING POWDER, 1 teaspoon
  • EGGS, 3
  • SUGAR, 1-1/4 cups
  • EGG YOLKS, 3

 

 

Directions: Sponge Cake

1.     Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.

2.    Grease and lightly flour an 8 by 11-inch sheet tray.

3.    Heat milk and butter until the butter melts.

4.    Stir the flour and baking powder together.

5.    Put the eggs, sugar, and yolks in a bowl and set over a hot water bath, whisking until the egg mixture triples in size.

6.    Fold in the flour mixture and the milk.

7.     Pour into the prepared pan.

8.    Bake for 10 minutes.
