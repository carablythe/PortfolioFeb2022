Tofu Muffins  (vegetarian with vegan version)

 

1 egg (omit for vegan version)

½ - ¾ C milk or soymilk

½ C tofu (¾ C tofu for vegan version)

1 T veg. Oil

2 C flour (wheat or white)

1/3 C sugar (or honey)

3 t baking powder

1 t salt

 

1)      Add 1 C cranberries, 2 C blueberries, or at least 2 bananas, etc.

2)    Add honey (or sugar for vegan version) to taste after all is mixed with fruit

 

Bake in greased muffin pans on 375º for 10-20 minutes.

 

 

Source: Donna Phillips
