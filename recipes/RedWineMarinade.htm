Red Wine Marinade (vegetarian/vegan version)

 

This Italian marinade is best with shrimp, chicken breasts or portabella mushrooms, to be eaten on pasta or with bruschetta.

 

 

3 T red wine (Chianti is best, but any red wine will do)

 

1- 1 ½  T red wine vinegar

 

3-4 gloves minced garlic

 

2 t – 1 T basil (fresh, if you’ve got it)

 

1 t Italian seasoning

 

½ t oregano

 

Sugar (for vegan version) or honey to taste

 

 

Mix the above ingredients and let the meat and/or veggies sit in the marinade for at least 30 minutes.  Best if cooked in the marinade on foil on the grill.
