New Orleans Creole Gumbo (with vegetarian/vegan versions)

 

1  28-oz. can of tomatoes (pureed, stewed, etc.)

1  8-oz. can tomato sauce (not paste)

1 medium green pepper, chopped

1 large onion, chopped

2 T  (or to taste) Tony Chachere’s Creole Seasoning, or other Cajun seasoning

2 bay leaves

1 t onion powder

1  10-oz. package frozen chopped okra (double for vegetarian/vegan versions)

¾ lb. of raw shrimp, shelled and deveined OR ½ lb shrimp, ½ lb. scallops (omit for vegetarian/vegan versions)

 

 

Put all ingredients except shrimp in crock-pot or large cooking pot.  Stir thoroughly.  Cover and cook on low heat, at least 1½ hours. Turn to high heat about 30 minutes before serving and stir in shrimp/scallops.  Serve over hot fluffy rice.

 

 

Adapted from the Crock-Pot Cookbook’s recipe.
