Anmitsu   (Vegan)

(yields 4 servings)

 

Ingredients:

 

One bar(piece) agar  (special gelatin made from seaweed, called "kanten" in Japanese.

         You can use gelatin if you cannot find agar, but it won’t be vegetarian.)

2 heaping T of sugar

One banana

½ C red bean jam (called "an" in Japanese)

Black honey  optional and not vegan

1 can fruit cocktail or whatever fresh fruits you desire

 

How to cook:

 

1. Soak agar in water.

2. When agar is getting soft, squeeze it and remove the surplus water because agar contains much water.

3. Tear agar into pieces.

4.  Put 450cc water (slightly less than 2 C) and torn agar in the pot and boil it to dissolve the agar.

    While boiling, keep stirring softly and add sugar.

5. Put dissolved agar in a case and leave it in refrigerator until it sets.

6. After it hardens, cut it into bite-sized pieces, like jello.

7. Put these pieces in a small bowl for each person.

8. Put sliced fruit or fruit cocktail over the agar.

9. Put some red bean jam on the agar.

10. If you have black honey, pour a little on top.

 

 

Source: Yuriko Aoki
