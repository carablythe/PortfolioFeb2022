Mom’s Quiche (with vegetarian version)

 

Blend the following together really well until smooth:

 

1/3 C tofu

2 lg. eggs

1 1/2 -1 3/4 C milk or cream

1-2 T of powdered milk (optional, to make it creamier if you use lowfat milk)

1 T flour

1/2 t nutmeg

2 t ground flaxseed (good for digestion, optional)

 

Later, you will add:

1 1/2 C cheese (really good if you combine swiss, cheddar, and mozzarella)

1 ½ C diced/chopped/sliced veggies of choice (I like broccoli, mushrooms, zucchinis, spinach…)

 

Grease the pan before putting the crust in (use whatever pie crust recipe you prefer…some on my website if you don’t have one you prefer) , gently prick the crust with a fork or knife in several places to keep out the air bubbles,

and bake the crust first at 200 degrees for about five minutes.

 

Then, add filling:  put the cheese and veggies in first, then pour the liquid mixture over top of the cheese and veggies.

 

Bake 375 - 400 degrees about 35-40 minutes

 

Source:  Donna Phillips
