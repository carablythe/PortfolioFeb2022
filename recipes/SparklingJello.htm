Sparkling Jell-o

 

Ingredients:

 

2 C sparkling wine（or apple/ white grape juice）　      

1/4 C seaweed gelatin flakes 　                                 　  　

2 T sugar　 　　　　　　　　 　　　                                          　　     

2/3 C fruit of choice (except kiwi or pineapple)

6 champagne glasses, small tea cups or jell-o molds

　　

Directions：

 

１.    Cut the fruit into small pieces and distribute equally into the glasses or molds.

２.    Tear 1/4 cup of gelatin with your hands, then blend in a food processor or coffee mill/grinder.

３.    Put the gelatin and the wine or juice in a medium-sized pot and wait about 10~15 minutes.

４.    Apply heat, and once heated, put in the sugar, and bring to a boil while stirring with a spoon.

５.    Stir until the gelatin and sugar are melted (about 5 minutes).

６.    Pour into the glasses or molds, and put them in the refrigerator.  Chill about 1-2 hours, then serve.
