Key Lime Pie (vegetarian)

 

 

1     8 oz. nonfat cream cheese

1 tsp. grated lime rind

1/3 c lime juice

¼ c sugar (or other sweetener)

1 – 8 oz. nonfat whipped cream topping

1 graham cracker crust

 

 

Beat everything except whipped topping. 

Fold that in and store in frig.

 

 

Source:  Laurel Phillips
