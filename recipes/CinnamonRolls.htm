Cinnamon Rolls

 

Icing ingredients:

2 tablespoons butter

1 cups powdered sugar

½  teaspoon vanilla extract

1 to 3 tablespoons hot water

 

Dough ingredients:

1.5 tsp. yeast

1/2 cup 35°warm water

1/2 cup scalded milk

1/4 cup sugar

1/3 cup butter/margarine

1 teaspoon salt

1 egg

3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour

 

Filling ingredients:

1/2 cup melted butter, plus more for pan

3/4 cup sugar

2 tablespoons cinnamon

3/4 cup raisins, walnuts (optional)

 

Pie filling directions:

 

1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and set aside.　

 

2. Put the milk in a small pot and while mixing with a spoon over heat until it almost boils (about 5 minutes).

 

3. In a large bowl mix milk, sugar, melted butter, salt and egg.　

 

4. Add 2 cups of flour and mix until smooth. 　

 

5. Add yeast mixture and remaining flour and mix until dough is easy to handle.

 

6. Knead dough on floured surface for 5 to 10 minutes.

 

7. Place in greased bowl, cover and let rise until doubled in size, for about 1 1/2 hours.

 

8. When doubled in size, punch down dough. Roll out on a floured surface into a large rectangle about 2cm thick.

 

9. Spread melted butter all over dough with your hands or a spoon. 　

 

10. Mix sugar, cinnamon, raisins and chopped walnuts. Sprinkle over buttered dough.

 

11. Beginning at the longer side of the rectangle, role up dough and pinch edge together to seal. Cut into 15 slices.

 

12.  Grease the bottom of the baking pan. Place cinnamon roll slices close together in the pan (it’s okay if they touch) and let rise until dough is doubled in size, about 45 minutes.

 

13. Heat oven to 350° F  (190° C).     

 

14. Bake for about 25 minutes, until golden.

 

15. Meanwhile, mix butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla. Add hot water 1 tablespoon at a time until the glaze reaches desired consistency. Lightly spread over slightly cooled rolls.
