                             Cornbread Stuffing

 

Ingredients:

　

·    Cornbread (see recipe)

·    2 cups of diced celery

·    2 cups of diced onion ½ cups chopped walnuts

·    1 cup diced apples (with peel or no peel, as you like)

·    5 T butter/margarine 

·    2 large eggs

·    1 T sugar

·    Salt and pepper, to taste

·    2 C warm water 

 

 

Directions:

 

1.    Let cornbread cool, and then crumble into a large bowl. 

 

2.    Sauté the onions and celery in 2 T of butter or margarine until golden brown.

 

3.    Mix in the onions and celery, eggs, sugar, salt, pepper, the rest of the butter/margarine, warm water to the bowl with cornbread crumbles.

 

4.    Place into a greased cake pan, cover it and place in 180 degree oven.  Check often and stir with spoon so it doesn't stick to sides or bottom.  Add a little more water if it is getting too dry.  45 minutes to an hour usually works.
