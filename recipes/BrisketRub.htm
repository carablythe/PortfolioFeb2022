Brisket rub   (sorry, no vegetarian/vegan version available)

Mix: 1 t celery salt, 1 t celery seed, 1 t onion salt, 1 t garlic salt, 1 t black pepper.  (Other seasonings could be substituted for any ONE of these, but not all)  Add 1 T worchestershire  sauce and 1 T liquid smoke to make a paste.   Rub all over brisket, cover, and refrigerate overnight or for several hours. 

Place brisket in pan with a little water, cover, and cook @ 300 degrees for 3 1/2 hours.  (Baste occasionally with juice)   Uncover, pour barbecue sauce over top, and cook for 1 1/2 hours. (Baste occasionally if wish to). 

Poke with knife to see if done.  Cook longer or remove from oven.  Cool a bit for easier slicing.  Save juice to pour over meat when reheating or eating.
