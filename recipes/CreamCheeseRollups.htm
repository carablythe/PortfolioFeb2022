Cream Cheese Roll-ups (vegetarian…no vegan version available)

 

Mix together:

one 8 oz.  package of cream cheese, softened

2-3 green onions, diced

 

Spread onto flour tortillas (about 10, medium-sized) and roll tightly. Squeeze gently from center to ends to make sure the cream cheese mixture is evenly distributed throughout the roll.

 

Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

 

Cut the rolls into 1-inch pieces and serve with salsa (see recipe), as dip.

 

An alternative to adding green onions, add 1 t ground fresh or powdered garlic,

1 t dried or fresh ground basil, and 2 teaspoons of grated parmesan cheese.

 

 

Source: Donna Phillips
