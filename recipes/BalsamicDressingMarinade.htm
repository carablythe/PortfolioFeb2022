Balsamic Vinegar Dressing/ Marinade

 

This is great as a salad dressing or as a marinade for fish, chicken breasts, mushrooms, eggplant, etc.

 

 

Ingredients

 

1 T balsamic vinegar

 

1 T white or rice vinegar

 

2 t lemon juice

 

2 t honey or sugar

 

1-2 garlic cloves, finely minced

 

1 T white cooking wine (for the marinade, not the dressing)

 

 

Directions

 

Mix the above ingredients well.

 

 

For the marinade, let the meat and/or veggies sit in the mixture for at least 30 minutes before cooking
