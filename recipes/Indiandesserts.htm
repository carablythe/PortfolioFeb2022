Indian desserts  (vegetarian with vegan version)

 

Rice Pudding:

 

1 C already cooked rice (any length grain)

1 C milk (or plain soy/rice milk for vegan version)

2 t almond extract

1 t vanilla extract

¼ C honey or 1/3 C sugar

¼ C ground almonds

 

 

OR

 

Banana Raita:  Mix toasted coconut and sliced bananas with ¾ C plain yogurt (or vegan plain yogurt if you can find it)
