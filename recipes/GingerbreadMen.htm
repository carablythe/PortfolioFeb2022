Gingerbread Men (and Women!)

 

 

Dry Ingredients:

 

3 cups flour

1 ½ teaspoon ginger powder

1 teaspoon cinnamon

1 teaspoon baking soda

½ teaspoon ground nutmeg

¼ teaspoon salt

 

Wet ingredients:

 

½ cup margarine/butter

½ cup brown sugar

1 egg

½ cup molasses

1 tablespoon cider vinegar

 

Icing (see recipe)

 

 

 

Directions:  

 

1.   Mix together the dry ingredients in a large bowl with a spoon.

 

2.Then, in a separate large bowl, mix together the wet ingredients with a mixer.

 

3. Now, mix the contents of both of these mixtures with an electric mixer until smooth. Separate the dough into four balls, cover with plastic wrap and put in the freezer for 20 minutes.

 

4.  After chilling, preheat the oven to 180 degrees C (350 F).

 

5. Roll the dough out on a lightly floured surface, to ¼ inch thickness. (Add more flour to roll out, if necessary.) Cut out the dough using cookie cutters or a knife and your own design. Place them at least 5 centimeters apart on the baking sheet. 　

 

6. Bake 7-9 minutes, or until crisp at the edges.  Let cool.  Repeat this process until all dough is used.

 

7.  Decorate with small candies, chocolate chips, dried fruit, etc. (Adhere all with the icing.)
