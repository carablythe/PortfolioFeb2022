Date and Nut Puffs (vegetarian…sorry, no vegan version yet)

 

3 egg whites

¼ t salt

1 ¾ C confectioners sugar

1 T all purpose flour

2 C chopped dates

2 C copped pecans

1 t vanilla

 

Beat egg whites with salt until stiff.  Fold in confectioners sugar and flour.  Add dates, pecans, and vanilla.  Line cookie sheet with foil. Drop batter on cookie sheet.  Bake in preheated 300º oven for 30 or 35 minutes. Let cool and peel off foil.

 

 

Source:  Evelyn Hartley, Houston Chronicle, July 2002.
