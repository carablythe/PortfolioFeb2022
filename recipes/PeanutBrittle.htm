Grandma’s Peanut Brittle (vegan)

 

1 C sugar

1C white karo

1 C hot water

2 C raw peanuts

2 t baking soda

 

Cook the sugar, karo and water together until it forms a hard ball when you put a little of the hot mixture in water… and it starts to crack when it hits the side of the pot.  Add peanuts and cook until mixture is golden brown.  Add the baking soda; stir well and pour onto a greased cookie sheet.  Let cool, and once room temperature, crack into pieces.
