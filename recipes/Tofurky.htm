Tofurky, Dressing/Stuffing, and Gravy (all vegan)

 

 

 Buy 5 packs of extra firm tofu, drained out all of the water, and then put all of the tofu in a food processor.  This smoothes out the tofu to the consistency of cream cheese. (This also how to make tofu cheesecake).  Then you put the tofu in several cheesecloths or lint-free towels and pad the tofu down in the cloth for a few minutes, absorbing the moisture.  The cloths will become very wet, so you need several layers.  You may want to separate the tofu before putting inside the cloth if you use 5 packs (which makes a LARGE tofurky).  Let the padded down tofu sit overnight, completely covered by the towels/cloth.

 

  In the morning (or at least six hours later), pull the cloth off of the tofu and throw the tofu BACK into the food processor.  Make sure it is again smooth, but this time it will be much thicker and may form balls.  Form all of the tofu around the stuffing, so that you can no longer see the stuffing and just the outer-layer of tofu.  Smooth the tofu with wet fingers.  You may want to sculpt this in an already greased 13x9" pan, or cookie sheet with a  rim.  Brush half of the basting over the exposed side of the tofurky.  Cover pan with tin foil.  Bake on 400 degrees for 30 minutes. Remove and put the rest of the basting on the tofurky.  Recover with foil and cook at the same temperature for another 15-30 minutes, or until brown and crisp (not TOO crisp...I forgot to cover mine with tin foil, and I cooked it too long, so mine was TOO crisp). 

 

  I will provide my mom's cornbread stuffing recipe below, but you may want to use whatever stuffing that you like best :).  Also, stuff the tofu with however much stuffing that will fit :) with at least 1/4" tofu layer all around.

 

My mom's stuffing recipe:

 

Make cornbread.  Get two boxes of a vegan cornbread mix (this is the easier way) or use a bag of cornmeal to make from scratch.  Recipe to make it from scratch is on the package of cornmeal (5 T blended firm tofu, soymilk or H2O, baking pwdr, salt, sugar to taste). If you are making it from cornmeal, I think a double recipe makes on 9 by 13 cakepan (It will say how much 1 recipe makes). Bake, cool, and crumble into a large bowl.  This makes A LOT of stuffing.  You may want to cut this recipe in half.

 

Dice 1-2 cups of celery, 1-2 cups of onion (depending on how much you want - I put lots of both) and mix it in with cornbread mix.  It is a little tastier if the onions and celery are sauteed in margarine before adding to the cornbread mix.  Beat 2 large eggs and mix them in.  Add salt and pepper to taste (and sugar to taste if it is bitter. A little sweetness goes a long way.) The BROTH is margarine (1/2 stick about) melted in 1 cup or more of water. Add it to the mixture.  You want it fairly soft because it will dry out as it cooks. Place the rest of what you don’t stuff in the tofurky in a greased pan, cover it and place in 375 degree or so oven.  Check often and stir with spoon so it doesn't stick to sides or bottom.  Add a little more water if it is getting too dry.  45 minutes to an hour usually works.

 

Basting

 

The basting is very simple.  For 5 packages of tofu, you need 1/4 cup of orange juice, 1/4 cup of soy sauce, and 1/4 cup of vegetable oil (I prefer olive).  If using less tofu, use proportionately less basting.

 

 

Gravy

 

1 stick of margarine

½ C flour

1 medium onion, finely diced

1 C soymilk (non-flavored)

1 C water

salt, to taste (at least ½ t)

 

Begin by sautéing the onions in about 1-2 t of the margarine.  Once the onions become golden, add the soymilk and about ¼ of the flour.  Continue to stir the mixture, adding the flour little by little and the water little by little.  Stir until smooth.  Season with salt. 

 

If you feel it needs more flavor, add some onion powder and maybe some garlic powder, to taste.
