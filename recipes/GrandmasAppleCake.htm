Fresh Apple Cake   (vegetarian…no vegan version available)

 

Mix together:

 

½ C vegetable oil

2 C sugar

2 eggs beaten

3 C flour

1 ½ t soda

1 t salt

1 t vanilla

add a little cinnamon, allspice, and/or nutmeg, if desired

 

Now add:

 

3 C diced apples and some chopped nuts, if desired

 

Mix everything together, squeezing with your hands.  The dough will be rather thick.

 

Bake at 350° in a bundt cake pan for at least 45 minutes (check with a toothpick)

 

Icing (make after the cake is out of the oven and cooling):

 

4 heaping T of powdered sugar

1-2 T of butter or margarine

1 t vanilla

pour in Evaporated Milk enough to thin (maybe 1 T?)

 

mix on high heat on the stove until smooth and liquidy.

 

Drizzle the icing over the cake.  May serve hot or after the icing as hardened a bit. J

 

 

 

Source:  Lorene Phillips
