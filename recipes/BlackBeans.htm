Black Beans and Rice   (Vegan)

 

1 can black beans

1 whole onion

1 T olive oil

2 T red wine vinegar

2 bay leaves

a splash of white wine  (I actually used more than a splash of Chianti once, instead, and it was delicious!)

2 cloves of garlic

1 t cumin

¼ C vegetable broth (optional)

 

 

Cook the above ingredients in a frying or sauce pan until the onions are transparent.  Mix with or serve on top of white rice.

 

 

Source:  Laura Vergot

 

 

Great with Fried Bananas on the side!  (The beans, not Laura ;)  .)
