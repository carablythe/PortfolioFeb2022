Decorating Icing

 

Ingredients:

 

1 ½ lbs (600 grams) powdered/ confectioner’s sugar

1 ½ cups flour

1 ½ teaspoon cream of tartar (or 1 tsp. lemon juice if you can’t find cream of tartar)

½ teaspoon salt

2/3 cup of margarine or butter  (or white vegetable shortening such as Crisco, if you can find it)

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 teaspoon almond extract (or another teaspoon of vanilla extract)

2/3 cups milk or soymilk

 

Directions:

 

1. Stir together the powdered sugar, flour, cream of tartar (if you use it) and salt.

 

2. Then add the shortening, extracts and milk. Mix thoroughly with an electric mixer.

 

3. Use to decorate gingerbread men, gingerbread houses, Christmas cookies and cakes. (Just add a few drops food coloring for desired colors.)

 

 

 

Source: Donna Phillips
