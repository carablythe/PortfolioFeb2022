Easy Peanut Brittle

 

Ingredients:

 

3 C sugar　　　

1 1/2 C water　

1 1/2 C peanuts (roasted, lightly salted okay)　

1 T Vegetable oil

 

You will also need a candy/ deep-fry thermometer for this recipe.

 

Directions:

 

1.    Coat the inside of a large-sized saucepan or pot with vegetable oil.

 

2.    Add the sugar and water to the saucepan and cook over high heat.

 

3.    Stir with a wooden spoon until it comes to a boil.

 

4.    Then, stop stirring, cover and continue to cook on high heat for 3 minutes.

 

5.    Take off the lid, reduce to medium high heat, and cook the mixture until a light amber color and at least 340 degrees F (170 degrees C). This could take just a few minutes or up to 30 minutes, depending on your stove.

 

6.    Once hot enough, quickly stir in peanuts and pour mixture onto a large baking pan or cookie sheet lined with greased wax or parchment paper (making a large rectangle – 0.5 cm high).

 

7.    Spread thin with a spatula on the paper as quickly as possible, but neatly.

 

8.    Cool completely (30 minutes to 1 hour) and then break into pieces of desired size.
