TZATZIKI SAUCE

 

Ingredients:

 

1/2 C. Plain Yogurt  
1/2 C. Sour Cream 
1 Cucumber  
1 tsp. ground Dill  
3 Garlic Cloves
2 T Red Wine Vinegar
1/4 C. Olive Oil
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Salt to Taste

 

Directions:

1. Combine cucumber, dill, garlic, vinegar, oil, sour cream and black pepper in a food processor. Blend well.

 

2. By hand, gently mix in yogurt.

 
3. Add Salt to taste.
