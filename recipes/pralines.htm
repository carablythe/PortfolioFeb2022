Grandma’s Pecan Pralines

 

Ingredients:

 

1 ½ C granulated sugar

1 ½ C brown sugar

2/3 C cream (or evaporated milk)

¼ tsp. salt

4 T light corn syrup (optional)

2 C broken pecans

1 t vanilla

1 T vegetable oil

 

Directions:

 

1.    Coat the inside of a large-sized saucepan or pot with vegetable oil.

 

2.    Add the sugars, cream, salt and corn syrup (if you use it) to the saucepan and cook over high heat.

 

3.    Stir with a wooden spoon until it comes to a boil.

 

4.    Boil to soft ball stage; test by dropping a small amount of the mixture in cold water (syrup will form a soft ball if ready).

 

5.    Remove from heat and add pecans and vanilla. 

 

6.    Beat by hand until it starts to thicken (1 minute or 2); drop from teaspoon onto greased wax or parchment paper. (Makes around 48 pralines.)

 

7.    Let cool completely, then remove from the paper.

　　

 

 

Source: Lorene Phillips
