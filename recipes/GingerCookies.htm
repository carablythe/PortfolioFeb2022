Ginger Cookies (vegetarian)

 

Mix these ingredients together:

2 cups flour

½ tsp. salt

1 tsp. cloves

1 tsp. ginger

1 tsp. cinnamon

3 tsp. baking soda

 

Mix in a separate bowl with beater until light and fluffy:

¾ cup vegetable shortening

1 cup sugar

 

Now add:

1 egg

¼ cup molasses

 

Then add the above flour mixture.

Roll into balls and cover in sugar. Bake at 350° for 8-10 minutes.

 

Source: Rebecca Koch
