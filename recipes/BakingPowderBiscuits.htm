Baking Powder Biscuits (vegetarian/vegan)

Yields 12 to 15 medium-sized biscuits

 

2 C flour

3 t baking powder

½ t salt

1/3 C cooking oil

3/4 to 7/8 C sour milk (if you just have regular or soy milk, add a teaspoon of vinegar to it)

 

1.      Sift flour, measure, and resift 3 times with baking powder and salt, the last time into a 3-qt. Mixing bowl. 

2.    Add oil and mix/shake mixture until particles are size of rice grains. 

3.    Add milk all at once and stir vigorously until dough just stiffens up. 

4.    Turn out onto floured board or pastry cloth, knead 12 to 15 times; then roll or pat out 3/8 to 3/4-inch thick (according to whether you want thin crusty biscuits or fat soft ones). 

5.    Cut out with biscuit cutter or open end of cup dipped in flour.

6.    Lift onto a greased baking pan.  Brush tops with melted butter or oil. 

7.    Bake in hot oven (450 degrees F) 10 to 15 minutes, depending on thickness, or until they are golden brown. 

Serve immediately.

 

Source:  Donna Phillips
