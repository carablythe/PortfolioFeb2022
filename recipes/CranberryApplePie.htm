Cranberry Apple Pie

 

 

Pie Crust

 

Ingredients:

 

· 3 cups flour

· 1 teaspoon salt

· 1/3 cup cold water 

· 2/3 cup butter/margarine

 

Filling

 

Ingredients:

 

·  2 cups cranberries (fresh or frozen)

·  2 medium or large apples (peeled and sliced)

· 1 cup sugar

· 1 cup water

· 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

· 1/4 cup orange juice

Filling Directions:

 

1.   Put cranberries, apples, sugar, cloves, water and orange juice in a pot and boil for 10 minutes…. or until apples are soft and cranberries have “popped” and congealed.

2.   Turn off the heat and set aside until the piecrust is made.

 

Pie Crust Directions:

 

1.     Mix together 2 cups of flour and salt. 

2.     Mix (1) with cold water to make a paste. 

3.     Then cut in butter or margarine until pea-sized balls form. 

4.     Cover both hands in flour. With your hands, form the dough into two large balls, one ball a little bigger than the other.

5.     Roll the bigger piecrust ball out on a flat, floured surface with a rolling pin to make a circle.

6.     Lay the circle into a greased pie pan and make small holes with a fork. Then, use your fingers (or a fork) to pinch the edge of the crust.

7.     Roll the smaller piecrust ball out on a flat, floured surface with a rolling pin to make a circle. Cut out your desired design (if any) with a table knife. 

8.     Set this top piecrust aside until the filling is in the pie.  ***Then, lay on top and use your fingers (or a fork) to pinch the edge of the crusts together until sealed.

 

Pie Directions:

 

1.     Add the filling to the crust.

2.     ***Put the top layer on the pie.

3.     Bake at 180°for 25 minutes

4.     Remove from the oven.

5.     Serve with vanilla cream or eat plain.  Enjoy!
