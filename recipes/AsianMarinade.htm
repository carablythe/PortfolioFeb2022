Asian Marinade (vegetarian/vegan)

 

This is great with tuna steaks (and other seafood, I imagine), chicken breasts, mushrooms, eggplant, what have you.

 

3 T soy sauce

 

2 T rice vinegar

 

2-3 T apricot or peach preserves (orange marmalade might be good, too)

 

3-4 cloves minced garlic

 

½ - 1 t grated fresh ginger or dried ginger

 

1 T mirin (cooking rice wine) or sake

 

2 t vegetable oil (I use olive)

 

 

Mix the above ingredients and let the meat and/or veggies sit in the marinade for at least 30 minutes.  Best if cooked in the marinade on aluminum foil on the grill.
