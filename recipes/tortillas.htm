Flour Tortillas

 

Ingredients:

 

3 cups flour

½ tsp. salt

¼ cup vegetable or olive oil for mixture

½ cup cold water

Vegetable or olive oil for frying

 

Directions:

1.    Combine the first four ingredients in a mixing bowl and stir with a spoon.

2.    Dip you hands in flour and mead until the mixture forms a smooth dough. (You may need to add more flour or more water to make dough that is soft and moist, but does not stick to your fingers.)

3.    Shape the dough into table-tennis-sized balls. Pat the dough flat with your hands and then take a rolling pin and roll into thin circles (thinner than naan).

4.    Add about ½ tsp of oil to the frying pan and put on medium heat.

5.    Once the oil is hot, add the tortilla, cook 1 minute, then flip.

6.    Cook another minute or until bubbles form or light brown circles appear.

7.    Repeat until all tortillas are cooked or store the tortilla dough in plastic wrap in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
