Fajitas

 

Ingredients:

 

·       Meat and Veggies:

·    3 tablespoons canola or vegetable oil

·    1 garlic clove, minced

·    1/2 onion, thinly sliced

·    1 teaspoon cumin

·    ½ tsp cayenne or other red pepper (powder)

·    salt, to taste  

·    200~250g of peeled shrimp, ground/shredded beef or ground/shredded chicken   

·    1 pack of button, maitake, or shimeji mushrooms, sliced

·    1/4 green bell pepper or 1 Japanese piman, thinly sliced    

·    1/4 red bell pepper, thinly sliced (optional)

·    1/4 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced (optional)

 

·    Refried or black beans (canned), heated   

·    Guacamole

·    Salsa  

·    Fresh shredded spinach or lettuce  　

·    Flour tortillas (1 pack or 10 homemade)   

·    Sour cream     

·    200 g shredded cheese  　

 

 

 

 

Directions:

 

1. Put the oil in a frying pan on medium-high heat.  　

2. Add the garlic and onions. Sauté until lightly browned.

3. Add the meat, cumin, cayenne/red pepper, and salt and cook thoroughly.

4. Now put in the bell peppers/piman and mushrooms and sauté the mixture for 3 minutes until the vegetables are limp.

5. Finally, add all ingredients to the tortilla, roll it and enjoy!
