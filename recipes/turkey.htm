Turkey

 

 

Directions:

 

1.    Defrost the turkey overnight in the refrigerator, bathtub or big bucket filled with ice water and 1 cup of salt. The next morning, once you see blood in the turkey’s plastic package or in the water, you will know it is defrosted.

 

2.    Once defrosted, remove the "innards".  Put them aside to be used for the gravy later.

 

3.    Now, rub the entire turkey with vegetable oil.

 

4.    Bake at 180 degrees for 40 minutes per 1 kg.  (Turkeys should be roasted the first 30 and last 30 minutes at 200 degrees.)
