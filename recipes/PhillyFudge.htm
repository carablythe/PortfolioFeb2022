Chocolate "Philly" Fudge (vegetarian)

 

4 c pwd sugar

1 8 oz cream cheese

4 - 1 oz sq. unsweetened chocolate, melted

1/2 c chopped nuts

1 t vanilla

dash of salt

 

Gradually add sugar to softened cream cheese, mixing until well blended.  Stir in remaining ingredients. 

 

Spread into greased 8 inch square pan & chill overnight.  Cut into squares.  Makes 1 3/4 lbs.

 

Source:  Sally Williams
