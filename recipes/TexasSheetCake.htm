Texas Sheet Cake (vegetarian)

 

 

Mix in pan:

 2 sticks (1 C) of butter

4 Tbsp. Cocoa and

1C water

 

Bring to boil; then pour into a large mixing bowl. 

 

Add:

2 C flour

2 C sugar

1/2C sour milk (if you just have regular or soy milk, add a teaspoon of vinegar to it)

1 tsp. baking soda

1 tsp. vanilla

2 eggs, slightly beaten

2 tsp. cinnamon

 

Mix well, pour into greased pan.  Bake at 400º F approximately 20-25 minutes.

 

Chocolate Icing

Begin making icing 5 minutes before cake is done. 

 

Melt together and bring to boil:

1 stick butter

4 Tbsp. milk

4 Tbsp. cocoa

 

Remove from heat and add:

 2C powdered sugar

1 tsp. vanilla

1C pecans 

 

Beat well.  Spread on cake while hot.
