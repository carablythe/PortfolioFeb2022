More About Me

I have been teaching English as a foreign language (EFL) in various contexts for a number of years now, though mainly in Japan and mainly at the university level. The past couple of years, however, I have felt fossilized in my work, that perhaps I have reached my full potential in the field of English language teaching. Therefore, I decided to shift to a more technical field where I can have the opportunity to pursue my interests, satisfy my need to create things and simply learn more.
The part of teaching I have always loved is actually such a small fraction of what my work has entailed: designing syllabuses, curricula, lesson materials, tests, assignments, etc. I have also proudly operated 100% paperless classrooms since 2017, during which time I have created multitudinous hybrid and online courses and run them using learning management systems and supplementary learning apps. I also enjoy coding, math, puzzles, languages, architecture, oil painting and really any kind of creative outlet or problem-solving activity!
If there is anyone who has a role I would be a good fit for, please kindly contact me! Thank you!
