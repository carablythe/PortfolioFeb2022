My Background

Hi! I'm Cara. I’m a nomad! During childhood and university, I lived in multiple locations throughout the U.S. including Texas, Colorado, Virginia and Pennsylvania. I've taught ESL/EFL in various contexts for quite some time, mainly in Japan as of late, but also in the U.S. when I was in graduate school, and in Namibia and Kiribati through the World Teach Program and the Peace Corps, respectively.

I love math, puzzles, languages, architecture, painting, music, and cooking and eating food of any country (the spicier the better!). It’s been a lifelong goal to incorporate as many of my interests as possible into my work, and I think I’ve made a solid effort thus far! But there are still a lot out of possibilities out there to explore.
